How Scores Are Calculated
All health scores are 0–100 and built from weighted dimensions computed directly from the live dataset. 🟢 70+ = Strong/Healthy · 🟡 45–69 = Watch/At Risk · 🔴 0–44 = Concern/Critical
Customer Health Score
DimensionWeightWhat It Measures
Profitability35%Customer margin vs portfolio average
Order Frequency25%Orders per active month (consistency)
Revenue Size25%Lifetime sales as percentile of all customers
Discount Independence15%Low avg discount = higher score (less price-sensitive)
Product Health Score
DimensionWeightWhat It Measures
Profit Margin40%Product margin vs category average
Discount Level30%Lower avg discount = higher score
Sales Velocity20%Revenue as percentile across all products
Order Consistency10%Unique order count (not one-off)
Region Health Score
DimensionWeightWhat It Measures
Profit Margin35%Region margin vs portfolio average
Sales Volume25%Revenue as percentile across regions
YoY Profit Growth25%Profit growth vs prior year (improving = higher score)
Discount Discipline15%Region avg discount vs portfolio avg
Sub-Category Score
DimensionWeightWhat It Measures
Profit Margin50%Sub-cat margin vs category average
Discount Discipline30%Avg discount vs portfolio average
Sales Velocity20%Revenue percentile across sub-cats
State Score
Profit Margin (50%) · Sales Volume percentile (25%) · Discount Discipline (25%)
All scores are normalised relative to the current dataset — a score of 70+ means this entity performs in the top tier for this specific dataset. Scores update automatically on every data refresh.
